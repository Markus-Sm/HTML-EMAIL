Go beyond the practice of meditation
 
As you may have discovered by now, Waking Up doesn’t only teach you how to meditate—we’ve also brought together some of the world’s most respected teachers, scientists, and scholars to help you explore what it means to live an examined, fulfilling, and truly good life.
